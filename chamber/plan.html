SpellSmart – Site Plan

Project planning document for the SpellSmart spelling and vocabulary platform.

Site Name

SpellSmart

This name reflects a smart, interactive platform for improving spelling and vocabulary. It is memorable, descriptive, and suitable for branding and recognition.

Optional domain: spellsmart.org

Site Purpose

SpellSmart is an interactive website that helps users improve their English spelling and vocabulary through daily challenges, spelling tests, and audio-assisted learning. It includes word definitions, pronunciation, and a progress tracker with badges to motivate learners.

Scenarios

  • How can I practice and improve my English spelling and vocabulary every day?
  • Where can I find my progress and earned badges for completed spelling challenges?

Color Schema

  • Primary Green (#7b993a): Used for headers, buttons, and highlights.
  • Accent Blue (#464fd3): Used for links, icons, and section headings.
  • Background Light (#f5f8ec): Used for page background and card backgrounds.

Typography

  • Work Sans – Used for main headings and section titles.
  • Roboto – Used for body text and navigation.
