Site Name

Kasoa Chamber of Commerce

This name reflects the official organization supporting local businesses and economic development in Kasoa. It is direct, professional, and clearly communicates the mission of the website.

Optional domain availability: kasoachamber.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Kasoa Chamber of Commerce website is to support business growth and community connections in Kasoa. The site will offer business directories, upcoming events, resources for entrepreneurs, membership information, and opportunities for networking and promotion.

Scenarios

  • How do I join the Kasoa Chamber of Commerce as a new business owner?
  • Where can I find a list of local businesses and services in Kasoa?

Color Schema

The website will use the following colors:

  • Primary Color: #aa00aa - Used for buttons, links, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #7462e0 - Used for background, navigation bar, and headings.
  • Accent1: #663399 - Used for the body.
  • Accent2: #7600a8 - Used for the paragraph tags.

Typography

The following fonts will be used on the site:

  • Headings: "Work Sans", sans-serif (Large, bold font used for titles and headings)
  • Body Text: "Roboto", sans-serif (Easy-to-read font for general text)
